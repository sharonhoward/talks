Six Petitions

  • Shortest: Letter/petition of Richard Dyer, 1733 (29 words + 2 for signature)
  • Longest: Petition of Joshua Perkin, Surveyor, 1793
  • Parish (appealing pauper removal): Petition of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St Mary White Chapel, 1781
  • Collective: Petition of the Journeymen Taylors working in the City of London and within five Miles thereof, 1770
  • Individual (professional?): Petition of Mary Burn, widow, 1715
  • Individual (more idiosyncratic): Petition of Elisabeth Exton, 1717
